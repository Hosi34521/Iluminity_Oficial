Security & Privacy Policy

Iluminity Inc. systems should only collect the information needed to complete a project request. Client data should be stored in secure platforms, protected with strong passwords, role-based access and limited team permissions. Sensitive information should not be sold or shared with third parties. Automations should be monitored to avoid sending incorrect messages. Websites should use HTTPS, secure forms, spam protection, privacy notices and clear consent for communications. For production projects, legal terms, cookie notices and privacy policies should be reviewed according to the client’s country and industry.

  • Role-based access for CRM users
